fuck my technology

You know those days when some piece of tech you own screws up your entire life? Maybe your cell phone drops a call, your car remote stops working, your PC (or Mac) decides you didn’t need to save any of your email, or your GPS decides to really tell you where to go?

This site is for those days—instead of going postal, just tell us your current techno-item of choice, and what it did to you this time, and just like that, it’ll be added to the Wall of Frustration. And trust us, reading about what technology is doing to other people can do wonders for your mood…
